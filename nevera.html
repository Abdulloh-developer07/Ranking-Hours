Rimac Nevera

The Rimac Nevera is an all-electric hypercar from Croatia, combining extreme speed with advanced technology.
🔹 Speed Record: Its top speed is 258 mph (412 km/h).
🔹 Engine & Power: Four electric motors delivering 1,914 horsepower and 2,360 Nm of torque.
🔹 Design & Aerodynamics: Sleek carbon fiber body optimized for downforce and battery cooling.
🔹 Production: Limited to 150 units, priced around $2.4 million.
🔹 Performance: 0–100 km/h in just 1.85 seconds, making it one of the quickest accelerating cars in the world.
In short: The Rimac Nevera redefines electric performance, blending sustainability with record-breaking speed.
