iPhone 15 vs Samsung Galaxy S24: Which Smartphone is Better in 2026?

Full real-world comparison after two weeks of heavy daily use including gaming, photography, battery tests and performance analysis.

Rating: 4.8/5
Published: February 3, 2026
Author: Abdulloh Mahmudov

Introduction

The flagship smartphone competition between Apple and Samsung continues to dominate the technology industry in 2026. Both companies released powerful premium devices with major improvements in camera technology, battery life, artificial intelligence features, and gaming performance.

The iPhone 15 and Samsung Galaxy S24 are two of the most popular smartphones in the world right now. Millions of users are trying to decide which one is actually worth buying.

To make this comparison as realistic as possible, both phones were tested for over two weeks in everyday situations including:

  • Gaming
  • Photography
  • Video recording
  • TikTok and Instagram
  • YouTube streaming
  • Multitasking
  • Travel and navigation
  • Battery endurance
  • Social media usage

Instead of only looking at specifications, this article focuses on real-world experience and long-term usability.

Design and Build Quality

Apple continues its premium minimalist design philosophy with the iPhone 15. The aluminum frame feels solid, comfortable, and elegant. The matte finish reduces fingerprints and gives the phone a clean professional look.

Samsung Galaxy S24 takes a more futuristic approach. The thinner bezels and brighter display make the phone feel modern and immersive.

Both devices are extremely premium, durable, and comfortable for daily use. Samsung looks more aggressive and modern, while Apple feels cleaner and more elegant.

Display Comparison

Samsung still dominates the smartphone display market. The Galaxy S24 includes a beautiful AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate which makes scrolling, gaming, and animations feel incredibly smooth.

The iPhone 15 display is extremely color accurate and perfect for watching videos, editing photos, and professional content creation. However, Apple still limits refresh rate on standard models.

Camera Comparison

Camera quality is one of the biggest reasons why users buy flagship smartphones. Apple and Samsung take completely different approaches to smartphone photography.

The iPhone 15 focuses on natural colors, stable video recording, and reliable performance in every situation. Social media apps are highly optimized for iOS, which makes the iPhone excellent for creators and vloggers.

Samsung Galaxy S24 focuses on hardware power and camera versatility. The 200MP sensor captures extremely detailed images while the advanced zoom system allows users to take impressive long-distance shots.

Camera Feature iPhone 15 Samsung S24 Winner
Main Camera 48MP 200MP Samsung
Video Recording 4K 60fps 8K 30fps iPhone
Zoom 2x 10x Samsung
Night Mode Good Excellent Samsung

If you care more about photography, zoom, and hardware power, Samsung is the better option. If you focus on video recording, social media, and natural colors, the iPhone performs better.

Battery Life Test

Battery life remains one of the most important features for modern smartphone users.

The iPhone 15 delivered around 6 to 7 hours of heavy screen-on time during testing. Apple’s optimization helps the battery stay stable and efficient.

Samsung Galaxy S24 performed noticeably better with its 5000mAh battery. The phone easily survived a full day of heavy use including gaming, navigation, streaming, and multitasking.

Samsung also supports significantly faster charging speeds.

Battery Feature iPhone 15 Samsung S24 Winner
Battery Size 3349mAh 5000mAh Samsung
Charging Speed 20W 45W Samsung
Daily Usage Good Excellent Samsung

Gaming Performance

Gaming performance on both devices is excellent. PUBG Mobile, Call of Duty Mobile, and Genshin Impact run smoothly at high graphics settings.

The iPhone 15 uses Apple’s A16 Bionic chip which remains one of the fastest smartphone processors available.

Samsung Galaxy S24 uses the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 processor paired with 12GB RAM. Combined with the 120Hz display, gaming feels smoother and more immersive.

Samsung also handles multitasking better while gaming.

Software Experience

iOS remains one of Apple’s biggest advantages. The ecosystem between iPhone, MacBook, Apple Watch, and AirPods is incredibly smooth and convenient.

Samsung’s One UI software offers more customization and flexibility. Users can personalize almost every part of the interface.

If you prefer simplicity and long software support, the iPhone is better. If you prefer customization and multitasking features, Samsung is the better option.

Price and Value

Both smartphones start around $799, but Samsung includes:

  • More RAM
  • More storage
  • Bigger battery
  • Faster charging
  • Better zoom camera

Apple focuses more on ecosystem quality, stability, and software optimization.

🏆 Final Verdict

After two weeks of intensive testing, the Samsung Galaxy S24 is the better overall flagship smartphone for most users in 2026.

It offers:

  • Better battery life
  • Superior display
  • Stronger zoom camera
  • More RAM and storage
  • Faster charging
  • Excellent gaming performance

However, the iPhone 15 remains an excellent choice for users already inside the Apple ecosystem or those who prioritize video recording and long-term software support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which phone has the better camera?

Samsung Galaxy S24 offers better zoom and photo detail, while iPhone 15 performs better for video recording.

Which smartphone lasts longer?

Samsung Galaxy S24 lasts significantly longer because of its larger battery.

Which phone is better for gaming?

Samsung Galaxy S24 provides a smoother gaming experience thanks to the 120Hz display and Snapdragon processor.
