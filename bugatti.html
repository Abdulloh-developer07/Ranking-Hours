Bugatti Chiron Super Sport 300+

see pictures
The Bugatti Chiron Super Sport 300+ is a record-breaking hypercar, the first production car to surpass the 300 mph barrier.
🔹 Speed Record: In 2019, it reached 304.77 mph (490.48 km/h), making history as the first car above 300 mph.
🔹 Engine & Power: 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 producing 1,577 hp, paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission and AWD.
🔹 Design & Aerodynamics: Features a “longtail” body for better airflow, lightweight carbon fiber, and signature orange stripes.
🔹 Production: Limited to 30 units, each costing about $3.9 million.
🔹 Performance: 0–100 km/h in 2.4 seconds. Customer cars limited to 440 km/h (273 mph) for safety.
In short:
The Chiron Super Sport 300+ set a new benchmark in automotive history, blending speed, power, and exclusivity.
