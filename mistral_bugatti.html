Bugatti Mistral

see pictures
The Bugatti Mistral, also known as the W16 Mistral, is a final tribute to Bugatti’s iconic W16 engine, designed as an open-top hypercar.
🔹 Speed Record: Projected top speed of 282 mph (454 km/h), making it the fastest roadster ever.
🔹 Engine & Power: 8.0-liter quad-turbo W16 producing 1,600 hp, carried over from the Chiron Super Sport.
🔹 Design & Aerodynamics: Open-top design, inspired by classic Bugatti roadsters, with advanced aerodynamics for stability at high speeds.
🔹 Production: Limited to 99 units, each costing around $5 million.
🔹 Performance: 0–100 km/h in about 2.5 seconds. Top speed estimated at 282 mph.
In short:
The Bugatti Mistral is a celebration of the legendary W16 engine, combining open-air luxury with record-setting speed.
