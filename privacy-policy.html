2. What Information Do We Collect?

To provide accurate product comparisons, review directories, and functional interactive tools, we collect different types of user data. This information falls into three distinct structural categories: data you give us voluntarily, data we collect automatically through tracking frameworks, and data provided by external third-party integration nodes.

2.1. Information You Provide directly to Us:

This includes any specific identification variables you share with us through manual form actions, text inputs, or direct outreach channels:

  • Contact and Communication Metrics: When you contact us using our contact forms or email, we collect your full name, email address, message subject, and the text content of your query.
  • Public Comments and Feedback: If you write comments under our product reviews, we collect the name or alias you provide, your email address, and your message text. Please remember that comment text is visible to anyone visiting the page.
  • Newsletter Subscription Records: If you sign up to get email alerts, product updates, or news from us, we store your email address and registration timestamp.

2.2. Information Collected Automatically:

As soon as you open our website, our server infrastructure logs standard browser connection details automatically:

  • Technical System Logs: We track your Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser version, device brand, screen resolution, operating system, network service provider, and country location.
  • Behavioral Analytics: We log data on how you navigate our site, including the specific pages you view, how long you spend on each article, your search queries, scrolling behavior, and the links you click.
  • Cookie Data Parameters: We use small alphanumeric text files stored on your local drive to remember your layout settings and help analyze our overall traffic patterns.

2.3. Information Collected via Third-Party Integrations:

We work with trusted digital networks to optimize our content structure and deliver relevant advertising:

  • Google Analytics Infrastructure: This service logs how visitors move across our site so we can continuously improve our layout and reading experience.
  • Google AdSense Advertising Network: AdSense uses behavioral cookies to show you tailored advertisements based on your previous web browsing history across the wider internet.
  • Social Media Referrals: If you click through to our platform from a social network link, that network may share anonymized referral data with our analytics dashboard.

3. Cookies and Advanced Tracking Technologies

Cookies are simple data files placed on your computer or mobile device by a website's server. They allow the platform to remember your unique actions and view preferences over time, saving you from re-entering your choices whenever you return or switch pages.

Our Comprehensive Cookie Usage Matrix:

Cookie Identifier Operational Purpose Lifespan / Retention Duration
session_id Maintains user navigation states across the active browser session. Temporary (Deleted upon closing browser)
user_preferences Remembers your site preferences, such as language settings and dark mode themes. 1 Year from deployment
_ga (Google Analytics) Distinguishes unique users to compile site traffic metrics. 2 Years from deployment
_gid (Google Analytics) Tracks daily visitor interactions to group page views. 24 Hours from deployment
__gads (Google AdSense) Serves tailored ads and prevents you from seeing the same ad too many times. 13 Months from deployment

How to Manage and Disable Cookies:

You can control, block, or delete cookies at any time through your web browser's built-in privacy settings. Please be aware that disabling essential cookies may impact your user experience and prevent some interactive features on our site from functioning correctly.

To change these settings in Google Chrome, go to: Settings → Privacy and security → Third-party cookies → and select "Block third-party cookies". For other browsers like Safari, Firefox, or Microsoft Edge, please consult their official help documentation to manage your tracking preferences.

5. Data Retention: How Long We Store Information

We only keep your personal details for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Our specific data retention windows are structured as follows:

  • Contact and Outreach Submissions: Retained for up to 2 years after your inquiry is resolved, ensuring we can reference past discussions if you need further help.
  • Analytics and Tracking Cookies: Automatically removed after 1 to 2 years, depending on the specific cookie settings defined in our tracking matrix.
  • Automated Server Log Files: Kept for exactly 3 months to monitor platform security, after which they are automatically overwritten.
  • Subscription Records: Kept safely in our database until you choose to unsubscribe via the link found in our emails.

Once these retention windows expire, your data is permanently deleted from our servers or fully anonymized so it can no longer be linked back to you.

10. Data Security Measures

We protect your information using a variety of administrative, technical, and physical security measures. These steps are designed to safeguard your data against accidental loss, theft, unauthorized access, or modification:

  • Secure Encryption: We use Hypertext Transfer Protocol Secure (HTTPS) and Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption to protect all data sent between your web browser and our servers.
  • Protected Infrastructure: Our website files are stored on secure hosting setups equipped with active firewall systems and automated intrusion detection.
  • Routine Security Scans: We run regular technical audits to look for software bugs, system vulnerabilities, and unauthorized access points.
  • Restricted Access Controls: Access to your personal data is limited to specific staff members who need it to keep our platform running smoothly.
  • System Backups: We create regular, encrypted database backups so we can safely restore our systems in the event of a technical emergency.

Please remember that while we use industry-standard security measures, no data transmission over the internet or digital storage system can be guaranteed 100% secure.
