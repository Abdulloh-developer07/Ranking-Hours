Introduction

Ranking-Hours participates in affiliate marketing programs that allow websites to earn commissions through referral links. Some pages on this website may contain affiliate links directing users to online retailers, marketplaces, software providers, or technology-related services. If users click these links and complete purchases, Ranking-Hours may receive a small commission from the retailer or affiliate platform.

These commissions help support the operation and growth of Ranking-Hours. Running a modern comparison website involves many expenses including hosting, design improvements, performance optimization, software subscriptions, testing equipment, content research, image creation, and long-term website maintenance.

Affiliate revenue allows us to continue producing free content without charging readers for access. Our goal is to create helpful and informative articles that assist users when researching smartphones, laptops, gaming products, software, consumer electronics, and modern technology.

Transparency is extremely important to our platform. Rather than hiding affiliate relationships, we openly explain how they work and how they support our ability to create high-quality content for readers around the world.

Editorial independence is one of the core principles behind Ranking-Hours. Affiliate relationships never directly influence article rankings, product ratings, opinions, recommendations, or final conclusions.

Brands, manufacturers, retailers, and affiliate programs do not control the content published on this website. Receiving commissions from affiliate links does not guarantee positive reviews or higher rankings for any product or service.

Products may still receive criticism if weaknesses, software limitations, poor value, performance issues, or usability problems are discovered during the evaluation process.

How Affiliate Links Work

Affiliate links contain unique tracking identifiers provided by affiliate networks or partner retailers. When a visitor clicks one of these links and later purchases a product or service, the retailer may pay a small referral commission to Ranking-Hours.

Importantly, affiliate commissions do not increase the price users pay. Customers generally pay the exact same amount whether purchases are made through affiliate links or directly through retailer websites.

Affiliate tracking systems often rely on browser cookies or temporary session tracking technologies. These systems help retailers identify which websites referred customers to their platforms.

Depending on the retailer or affiliate program, tracking periods may vary. Some affiliate programs only track purchases for a few hours while others may track purchases for several days after users click referral links.

Additional details regarding cookies, tracking technologies, and user privacy can be found within our Cookies Policy and Privacy Policy pages.

Smartphones, laptops, gaming consoles, headphones, software tools, and automotive technologies are evaluated based on how users actually interact with them during everyday life.

Recommendations may evolve over time as new products are released, software updates improve performance, or market conditions change.
