Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut

see pictures
The Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut is designed to be the fastest Koenigsegg ever built, optimized for maximum top speed and aerodynamics.
🔹 Speed Record: Claimed top speed over 310 mph (500 km/h). Official test still awaited but aerodynamically capable.
🔹 Engine & Power: 5.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine producing up to 1,600 hp (on E85 fuel) with a 9-speed Light Speed Transmission.
🔹 Design & Aerodynamics: Longtail design with minimal drag (Cd 0.278). Sleek, futuristic body optimized for high stability at extreme speeds.
🔹 Production: Limited numbers planned. Price estimated around $3 million.
🔹 Performance: 0–100 km/h in about 2.6 seconds. Target top speed: 310+ mph.
In short:
The Jesko Absolut is Koenigsegg’s ultimate speed machine, built to push beyond 300 mph and claim the crown of the fastest car in the world.
