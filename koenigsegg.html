Koenigsegg Regera

see pictures
The Koenigsegg Regera is a hybrid hypercar that combines extreme power with luxury and innovative technology.
🔹 Speed Record: Top speed is about 250 mph (402 km/h).
🔹 Engine & Power: 5.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine with three electric motors, producing 1,500 hp.
🔹 Design & Aerodynamics: Sleek body with active aero and lightweight carbon fiber chassis.
🔹 Production: Limited to 80 units, priced around $2 million each.
🔹 Performance: 0–400 km/h in 20 seconds, one of the fastest accelerating cars in the world.
In short: The Koenigsegg Regera blends raw power with hybrid efficiency, redefining hypercar performance.
