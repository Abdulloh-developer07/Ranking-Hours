Aston Martin Valkyrie

see pictures
The Aston Martin Valkyrie is a Formula 1-inspired hypercar developed with Red Bull Racing.
🔹 Speed Record: Top speed is about 250 mph (402 km/h).
🔹 Engine & Power: 6.5-liter naturally aspirated V12 paired with hybrid system, producing 1,160 hp.
🔹 Design & Aerodynamics: Aggressive aero-focused design with F1-level downforce.
🔹 Production: Limited to 150 road cars, priced around $3 million.
🔹 Performance: 0–100 km/h in 2.5 seconds.
In short: The Valkyrie is Aston Martin’s ultimate hypercar, bringing Formula 1 technology to the road.
