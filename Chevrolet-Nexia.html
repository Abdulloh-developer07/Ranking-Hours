Chevrolet Lacetti wins overall because of stronger reliability, better resale value, cheaper maintenance, and a more proven long-term ownership experience.

Long-term reliability still slightly favors Lacetti, but Nexia 3 continues improving every year with better materials, upgraded sound insulation, and newer technology integration.
