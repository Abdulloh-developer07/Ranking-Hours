McLaren Speedtail

see pictures
The McLaren Speedtail is the fastest McLaren ever built, a futuristic hyper-GT blending speed and luxury.
🔹 Speed Record: Top speed is 250 mph (403 km/h).
🔹 Engine & Power: 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 with hybrid assistance, producing 1,035 hp.
🔹 Design & Aerodynamics: Teardrop shape with extreme aerodynamics for maximum efficiency.
🔹 Production: Only 106 units, each costing about $2.25 million.
🔹 Performance: 0–300 km/h in 13 seconds.
In short: The Speedtail pushes McLaren’s limits, offering both high performance and futuristic design.
