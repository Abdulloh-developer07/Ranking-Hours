The SSC Tuatara is an American hypercar developed by SSC North America, aiming to challenge the world’s fastest cars.
🔹 Speed Record: Reached 295 mph (475 km/h) in real-world runs, with disputed higher claims in 2020.
🔹 Engine & Power: 5.9-liter twin-turbo V8 producing up to 1,750 hp when fueled with E85.
🔹 Design & Aerodynamics: Designed with a Cd of 0.279, sleek futuristic lines, and aerospace-inspired aerodynamics.
🔹 Production: Limited to 100 units, price around $1.9 million.
🔹 Performance: 0–100 km/h in under 2.7 seconds. Projected max speed ~300 mph.
In short:
The SSC Tuatara stands as a bold American rival to Bugatti and Koenigsegg, combining extreme aerodynamics with massive power.
