Hennessey Venom F5

see pictures
The Hennessey Venom F5 is an American hypercar engineered for one goal: to exceed 300 mph and become the fastest road car in the world.
🔹 Speed Record: Claimed top speed of 311 mph (500+ km/h). Official verified top speed run is still awaited.
🔹 Engine & Power: 6.6-liter twin-turbo V8 “Fury” producing 1,817 hp and 1,193 lb-ft of torque.
🔹 Design & Aerodynamics: Ultra-light carbon fiber body (1,360 kg). Designed with minimal drag and maximum stability for record-breaking speed.
🔹 Production: Only 24 units, priced at around $2.1 million each.
🔹 Performance: 0–100 km/h in 2.6 seconds. Projected top speed: 311 mph (502 km/h).
In short:
The Venom F5 is America’s answer to Bugatti and Koenigsegg, created to break the 300 mph barrier with unmatched raw power.
