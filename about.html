Our Story

It all started in 2015. I (Abdulloh) wanted to buy a new laptop. I read 100 reviews online, but everyone was saying something different. One said "It's amazing," another said "It's terrible."

I ended up buying 3 laptops myself, tested them for a week, kept the best one, and returned the rest. When my friends heard about this, they started asking me: "Abdulloh, is this any good?"

That is how I launched Ranking Hours in 2025. My goal was simple: to give people honest advice in plain language. We work for the users, not for the corporations.

How Do We Work?

1

Acquiring Products

We source products directly from retail outlets or get testing units from companies. No corporate biases allowed.

2

Real-World Testing

We live with the product for 1 to 4 weeks. Our process focuses on real-life application, not just benchmark numbers.

3

Data Collection

We log daily metrics meticulously: actual battery performance, real-world camera clarity, and ergonomic comfort.

4

Writing the Review

Once evaluation wraps up, we format results into conversational text covering pros, cons, and target buyers.

5

Community Engagement

We publish our articles and actively reply to reader questions. If an oversight is found, we swiftly update it.
